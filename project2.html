Introduced in 2011, Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim sold over thirty million copies and provides dozens of hours of entertainment for those hearty souls that visit the snowy province of Cyrodiil.

While the Dragonborn hero you play levels up in the game, can you "level up" while playing?

Using simple bodyweight exercises and concepts from Jane McGonigal's Superbetter, you to can forge the strength necessary for surviving the harsh challenges of Skyrim!
