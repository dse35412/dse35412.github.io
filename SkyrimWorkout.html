Introduced in 2011, Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim sold over thirty million copies and provides dozens of hours of entertainment for those hearty souls that visit the snowy province of Cyrodiil. Tom Bissell reviewed the game extensively for Grantland.

While the Dragonborn (all will be revealed during in-game quests) hero you play levels up in the game, can you "level up" fitness-wise while playing?

Using simple bodyweight exercises and concepts from Jane McGonigal's Superbetter, you too can forge the strength necessary for surviving the harsh challenges of Skyrim!

*please see a physician before starting any fitness program

GAME LEVEL EXERCISE PROGRAM

As you endeavor to survive in Skyrim, you get tested in a variety of skills. As your skills improve, you get to level up and choose to increase your Magika(magic ability), Health(ability to withstand attacks and damage) and Stamina(ability to carry item, wear armor and perform movements over time). Every time you level up your character, based on the attribute you raise do one set of the corresponding exercise. If you really want to go for, it do a set for every level you achieved!
